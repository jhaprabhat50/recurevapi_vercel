What We Do

Comprehensive Physiotherapy & Rehab Services

Our clinic offers specialized treatments to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve quality of life through evidence-based therapies.

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Customized recovery plans for joint replacements, fractures, and arthritis to restore function.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Therapy for stroke, Parkinson’s, and spinal cord injuries to improve mobility and independence.

Geriatric Rehabilitation

Tailored programs for seniors to enhance balance, strength, and daily living activities.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised exercises and education for heart disease recovery and prevention.

Cardio-Respiratory Rehabilitation

Improve lung function and endurance for COPD, asthma, and post-COVID recovery.

Occupational Rehabilitation

Restore work capacity after injuries with job-specific training and ergonomic adjustments.

Community Rehabilitation

Home-based or group therapies to reintegrate patients into daily life post-injury.

Post-Surgical Care

Accelerate recovery after surgeries (e.g., ACL repair, joint replacements) with guided rehab.

Sports Injuries

Rehab for sprains, strains, and fractures to safely return to athletic activities.

Acute & Chronic Pain

Multimodal approaches (manual therapy, exercises) to manage persistent pain.

Musculoskeletal Pain

Targeted relief for muscle, tendon, and joint pain through evidence-based techniques.

Back Pain, Neck Pain, Sciatica

Spinal adjustments, traction, and exercises to alleviate nerve compression.

Ergonomics

Workspace assessments to prevent repetitive strain injuries and improve posture.

Biomechanical Correction

Gait analysis and alignment therapies to address movement imbalances.

Osteopathy

Gentle manipulations to restore balance in muscles, joints, and connective tissues.

Taping

Kinesiology or rigid taping to support joints and reduce swelling post-injury.

Dry Needling

Trigger point therapy to release muscle tension and improve blood flow.

Pilates

Low-impact exercises to strengthen core muscles and enhance flexibility.

Strengthening & Reconditioning

Customized exercise programs to rebuild strength after illness or inactivity.

About Dr. Mittal V Baish (PT)

Committed To Your Long-Term Well-Being

Dr. Mittal Baish is a licensed physiotherapist, osteopath and chiropractic specialist. She holds a Bachelor of Physiotherapy from Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth (Pune), a Diploma in Osteopathy & Manipulative Theory and Practice from the Osteopathic College of Ontario, and a Diploma in Master of Chiropractic from The Ackermann College. Certified in Dry Needling and Pilates Level 1, she combines multiple hands-on techniques to deliver holistic, patient-centred care.
